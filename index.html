Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the right courses for me?

Edify provides you with a survey like questionnaire where you'll answer all the questions and in the end. The result will show some courses you can directly apply or can contact our experts.

Do I get a Certificate for every course I take?

After completion of courses, the course will give a project to complete and fulfill other requirements to certify you. Edify certificates has been approved by approx. 9L companies across India and global which can help you acquire your dream job.

Do I get a refund if the course isn't worth my money?

Edify provides you with a 3-month money-back-guarantee. In the starting 3-months, if you feel like the course isn't for you, you can apply for the refund.

Will I have a lifetime access to the courses I purchase?

Courses available on Edify will be accessed to you for lifetime without any extra charges, so that you can refresh your knowledge or can update to the changes done in the course.

Will you assist me to get my first job?

Courses on Edify only provides you with certification which is approved by 9L companies across India and global. Edify doesn't provide any placements, but courses offers you with Interview questions and various small practice projects.

What are your payment options?

Edify accepts all credit cards, debit cards, and also netbanking payment options.

What are the requirements to start?

Some courses in Edify requires some sort of knowledge before-hand but major projects doesn't require any prior knowledge, you can just enroll and get started as the courses are beginner friendly.

Do I get all source code from projects?

Courses in Edify provides you with git repositories and various sources for assets and so on. Even practice project codes are provided, so that you can learn without any stoppage. We encourage that the learner first solves the given problem first-hand and then check for the solutions in source code.
